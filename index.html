Guide

How do I add tasks?
+

Type directly in the quick-add field, or click "New Task" for the modal experience. Press Enter to submit instantly.

Are tasks saved automatically?
+

Yes. Every action is instantly saved to your browser's localStorage, persisting across sessions.

What are the keyboard shortcuts?
+

Press Ctrl+K (Cmd+K on Mac) to open the task modal. Press Escape to close it. Enter submits forms.

How do I complete tasks?
+

Click the checkbox beside any task. The statistics update in real-time to reflect your progress.
